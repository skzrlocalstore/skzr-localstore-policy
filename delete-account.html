Delete Account – SKZR LocalStore

SKZR LocalStore respects your privacy and gives you full control over your account and data. If you wish to delete your account, you can request account deletion using the method below.

How to request account deletion

To delete your SKZR LocalStore account, please submit a deletion request using the form below:

Request Account Deletion

Data that will be deleted

  • User profile information (name, email)
  • Saved addresses
  • Order history
  • App usage data linked to your account

Data that may be retained

Certain data such as transaction records may be retained for legal or accounting purposes for a limited period, as required by applicable laws.

Deletion processing time

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 working days. Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered.
