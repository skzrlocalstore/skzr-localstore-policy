Privacy Policy - SKZR LocalStore

Last Updated: 02 December 2025

SKZR LocalStore (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates the SKZR LocalStore mobile application. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and Google UID when signing in.
  • Contact Details: Delivery address and phone number.
  • Order Data: Products ordered, order value, timestamps, payment confirmation.
  • Device Info: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token for push notifications.
  • Usage Data: Crash reports, analytics, and log data to improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your SKZR LocalStore account.
  • To process, fulfill, and deliver your grocery orders.
  • To send push notifications regarding order updates.
  • To notify you about new products, offers, or promotions.
  • To improve app performance, analytics, and security.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share limited information with:

  • Delivery partners (name, address, phone) to deliver your order.
  • Payment gateways (UPI/Razorpay) to complete transactions.
  • Firebase services for authentication, notifications, and database management.

4. Push Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send order status updates and promotional alerts. You can disable notifications anytime from your device settings.

5. Data Security

We use secure, industry-standard technologies including HTTPS and Firebase Security Rules to protect your personal information. However, no online service is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You may request to update or delete your account information by contacting us.

7. Children’s Policy

SKZR LocalStore does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
