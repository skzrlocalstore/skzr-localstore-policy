Refund & Cancellation Policy - SKZR LocalStore

Last Updated: 02 December 2025

1. Order Cancellation

  • You may cancel your order before it is accepted or prepared by the store.
  • Once an order is “Out for Delivery,” cancellation is not allowed.
  • We reserve the right to cancel orders due to unavailability, address issues, or payment errors.

2. Refund Policy

  • Refunds are applicable only for prepaid orders that get cancelled.
  • Refunds will be processed back to your original payment method (UPI/Razorpay/etc.).
  • Refund initiation may take 24–72 hours depending on the payment provider.
  • Damaged, spoiled, or missing items must be reported within 2 hours of delivery.

3. Non-Refundable Items

  • Fresh produce, dairy, and perishable items are not refundable unless damaged/spoiled.
  • Items with broken packaging after delivery are not eligible for refund.

4. Replacement Policy

  • For defective or incorrect items, we may offer replacement or refund as per availability.
  • Replacement is subject to stock availability.

5. Delivery Issues

  • If delivery fails due to wrong address or unreachable phone, no refund is provided.
  • If our delivery partner fails to deliver due to their issue, we will refund or redeliver.
